Meet Recap

Our first app — monthly status reports for Jira, written from the work you actually completed.

Recap For Jira Cloud

One click turns your completed Jira work from the last 30 days into a written monthly status report — grouped by epic, in plain past tense, ready to paste into a status update, a 1:1, or a performance review.

  • No blank page — the report writes itself from real work.
  • No invented metrics — only what actually shipped.
  • Runs entirely on Atlassian — one read-only permission, stores nothing.
